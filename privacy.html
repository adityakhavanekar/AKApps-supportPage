Privacy Policy

Motor AI - Last Updated: November 23, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Motor AI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our app may collect the following types of information:

  • Camera/Photo Data: Images you capture or select for processing within our app
  • Usage Analytics: App usage statistics to improve performance and user experience
  • Device Information: Basic device and iOS version information for compatibility
  • Subscription Data: Purchase and subscription information managed by Apple

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide core app functionality (AI-powered car recognition)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand how our app is used to enhance user experience
  • Provide customer support when requested
  • Comply with legal obligations

AI Processing

When you use the car identification feature:

  • Images are sent to AI service providers (Google Gemini or OpenAI) for recognition processing
  • Images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on external servers
  • Results are returned to your device and stored locally if you choose to save them

Data Storage

Your data is stored as follows:

  • Local Storage: Scan history and favorites are stored locally on your device using CoreData
  • iCloud: If enabled, data may sync via iCloud according to your device settings
  • We do not maintain servers that store your personal car scan data

Advertising

Our app does not currently display third-party advertisements. If this changes in the future, we will update this privacy policy accordingly and ensure that any advertising partners comply with privacy standards.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Images processed for AI recognition are not permanently stored on external servers
  • Data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS/TLS)
  • We follow Apple's security guidelines and best practices
  • Access to user data is limited to essential app functionality only

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Limited sharing may occur only in these circumstances:

  • AI Processing: Images are sent to AI service providers for recognition processing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist in app operation (under strict privacy agreements)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your locally stored data by uninstalling the app
  • Clear your scan history and favorites within the app
  • Deny camera or photo library permissions at any time via iOS Settings
  • Request information about what data we process

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • Significant changes will be communicated through app updates
  • Continued use of our app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
